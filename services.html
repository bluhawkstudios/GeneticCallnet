Service FAQs

Which Hiring Solution Is Right for You?

What is the difference between RPO and permanent recruitment?

Permanent recruitment fills specific roles with qualified long-term hires. RPO is an embedded recruitment model in which Genetic Callnet operates as an extension of your HR team and manages part or all of the hiring lifecycle.

When should a business use contract staffing?

Contract staffing is useful when you need skilled professionals for project-based, seasonal, temporary, or rapidly changing workforce requirements without making every role a permanent hire.

Does Genetic Callnet support international hiring?

Yes. Our global recruitment team supports cross-border hiring across the UAE, Europe, Asia, and other markets with talent sourcing, compliance coordination, cultural alignment, and relocation support.
