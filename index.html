Frequently Asked Questions

Recruitment Questions, Answered

What recruitment services does Genetic Callnet provide?

Genetic Callnet provides permanent recruitment, executive search, Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO), contract staffing, global recruitment, turnkey recruitment projects, recruitment advisory, training and development, corporate HR events, and employee wellbeing programs.

Where does Genetic Callnet operate?

Genetic Callnet is headquartered in India, has offices in the UAE, and supports organizations hiring across India, the UAE, Europe, Asia, and other global markets.

Can Genetic Callnet help with senior and leadership hiring?

Yes. Our executive search and permanent recruitment teams specialize in senior, leadership, and high-impact roles across finance, technology, manufacturing, retail, entertainment, and other sectors.

How can a business speak with a recruitment expert?

Share your hiring requirements through the contact page or start a conversation with Genetic Callnet on WhatsApp. Our team can help define the role, hiring model, timeline, and market approach.
